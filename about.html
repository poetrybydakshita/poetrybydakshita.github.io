About Me

Hi, I’m Dakshita Dhingra — a writer, poet, and storyteller who believes that emotions deserve honest words. I’ve spent years turning feelings, memories, and unspoken thoughts into poetry and prose that people can feel, not just read.

Writing became my way of understanding the world — of loving deeply, fighting quietly, and healing softly. Through Poetry by Dakshita, I create personalised poetry, meaningful words, and creative writing that holds space for real emotions.

Whether it’s love, loss, hope, or becoming — my work is for those who feel deeply but struggle to explain it. If words matter to you, you’re in the right place.
