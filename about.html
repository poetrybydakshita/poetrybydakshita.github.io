About Me

Hi, I’m Dakshita Dhingra — a writer, poet, and author who believes that emotions deserve honest words. I’ve been writing for over seven years, turning feelings, stories, and moments into poetry and meaningful writing.

Through Poetry by Dakshita, I offer writing services, freelancing, and customised poetry crafted from real emotions — for those who feel deeply but struggle to express it.
